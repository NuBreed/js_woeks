    about us

    St. Mary’s Convent Sr. Sec. School, Dhampur Belongs To The Nav Jyoti Education Society, Meerut, A Religious And Charitable Registered Body, Under The Registration Act Of Xxi 1860 And The Article 29 And 30 Of The Constitution Of India. It Is Under The Patronage Of The Catholic Bishop Of Meerut Diocese, Who Also Is Its President. St. Mary’s Senior Secondary.
